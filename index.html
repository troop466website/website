About Us

Transforming young people into confident leaders through outdoor adventure and community service

The Spirit of Adventure

Every Eagle Scout's journey begins with a single step. Through challenging outdoor adventures, we build character, develop leadership skills, and create memories that last a lifetime. Just like the determined climber scaling the mountain peaks, our scouts learn that with perseverance and teamwork, any summit is achievable.

"The summit is what drives us, but the climb itself is what matters most"

Commitment to Excellence

Troop 466 Scouts and leaders have earned most of the highest awards in Scouting. Nearly a dozen Troop Eagle Scouts have earned their Silver Palm (15 merit badges beyond Eagle). One Scout earned the Adams Gold Award for the best Eagle service project in the Western United States. Another Scout earned the Medal of Merit for saving a life.

Silver Palm Eagles
Adams Gold Award
Medal of Merit

Commitment to Innovation

Troop 466 was selected as both an alpha and beta site for the Boy Scouts of America's new Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) initiative, pioneering new ways to engage scouts in technical learning and innovation.

BSA STEM Pioneer
